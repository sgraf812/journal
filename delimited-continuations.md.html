# Thoughts about delimited continuations See [Wikipedia](). ## shift/reset In a nutshell, as a refresher: ```scheme (+ 1 (reset (* 2 (shift k (k (k 4)))))) ``` returns 17; is just the continuation between `reset` and `shift`, which is called *twice* in the `shift`ed expression (which is evaluated before the continuation, as per CbV). After evaluation of the expression, execution continues outside of the reset, increments to 17. The occurrence of `shift` marks the hole of the continuation's execution context.
